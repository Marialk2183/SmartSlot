Welcome to Mukesh Patel School of Technology Management and Engineering

Established in 2006 as a part of NMIMS, MPSTME is pioneering in integrating technology education with management practices, setting standards in engineering and technological advancements.

Our Vision and Mission

Our vision is to lead in the field of technological education, merging technical expertise with management skills to prepare students for global challenges. Our mission emphasizes innovation, hands-on learning, and a commitment to transforming students into leaders of tomorrow.

State-of-the-Art Infrastructure

Our campus boasts modern laboratories and classrooms, equipped with the latest technology to support an engaging learning environment. Our commitment to comprehensive education ensures that our graduates are ready to meet industry demands.
